There is one thing you are never too busy to do and that is taking time to make a budget.

What is a budget?

A budget is a financial plan that will help you make smart spending choices today while preparing for tomorrow.

Budgeting will help you achieve your goals and prepare you for unexpected bumps in the road.

So what goes into making a budget?

First, you'll need to track your income and expenses for a particular period of time, such as a month. You can do so by Adding A Project and Getting Started.

After that, making your budget comes down to a simple equation: what you earn - what you spend.

To determine your expenses include items such as tuition, books, rent, credit card payments and car payments Then simply subtract your expenses from your income.

Are your earnings greater than your spending?

If so , you're on the right track and if not, Budget Tracker will try to make it easier for you and help show you how much you're spending and you can then determine how to balance your budget.

Use Budget Tracker to be mindful of your daily expenses, that way you can make sure you're spending your money on things that really matter to you.When you budget you decide where your money goes instead of wondering where it all went.
